What is Boreas Hill?

Boreas Hill is my work-in-progress novel (named after the main character) that I have been working on since November 2023. I've been working hard to get the first draft done, and I hope to be a published author one day.

Boreas Hill Logline

Once Boreas Hill finds out about his elemental powers, him and his brother must learn to survive by themselves in one of the few places on Earth that are safe for them. After adjusting, Boreas must find the culprit to the deaths and robberies around town, all while mastering his newfound elemental powers and proving himself to the people around him. All art for the Boreas Hill universe is done by Margo Margan Support Margo!
